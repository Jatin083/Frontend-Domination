⭐ Simple Animation ⭐

Example:-

 ✨ Initial se final ki or: ✨
 gsap.to("#box", {
     x: 1000,
     rotate: 360,
     duration: 2,
     delay: 2,
     backgroundColor: "pink",
 });

 ✨ Final se initial ki or: ✨
 gsap.from("#box", {
     x: 1000,
     rotate: 360,
     duration: 2,
     delay: 2,
     backgroundColor: "pink",
 });
